## Budget Tracking
- Built a **budget tracking React app** to monitor expenses based on a predefined budget.
- Features include:
  - Adding budget and expenses.
  - A **progress bar** to visualize expense percentage.
- Technologies used: **React.js, Bootstrap, JavaScript, JSX, HTML, CSS**.
